You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from our application. This page explains how to delete your data from Jeep PR.

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Delete Your Account (Recommended)

  1. Open the Jeep PR mobile application
  2. Go to your Profile settings
  3. Look for "Delete Account" or "Remove Account" option
  4. Follow the prompts to permanently delete your account
  5. This will delete all your personal data from our systems

Method 2: Contact Support

  1. Send an email to our support team
  2. Include your email address used for the account
  3. Request complete data deletion
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and account information
  • Your profile data (name, profile picture)
  • Your saved routes and preferences
  • Your location history (if any)
  • Your usage statistics and app analytics

Alternative: Disconnect Facebook Login

If you only want to disconnect Facebook login but keep your account:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "Jeep PR" in your connected apps
  4. Click "Remove" to disconnect
